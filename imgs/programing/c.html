Battle for computer geeks.
Platform to excel your programming skills and to conquer the coding era!

Event Description

This is a competition not only for coders but for “elegant” and “efficient” programmers.
Plunge in to unravel the deep C secrets and swim up to the shore with the best algorithm. This is your chance to test your "programming nerves" by fighting it out with the better, to be the best!

Instructions

  1. Team Size: 2 (max)
  2. Each Team will be provided a System in each round.
  3. Utilizing any kind of electronic aid will lead to disqualification.
  4. Last Date for Registration: 14th September 2013.

Rules and Regulations

    The event will be held in three rounds.

  1. First Round
    Check your IQ with MCQ.

    1. This is a preliminary round consisting of multiple choice questions.
    2. Time Limit: 40 Minutes
    3. There will be negative marking for wrong answers.

  2. Second Round
    Debug the Bug.

    1. This is a Debugging round.
    2. The participants will be provided with erroneous programs .The candidates will have to face the challenge of debugging them.
    3. Time Limit:45 Minutes

  3. Third Round
    Decode the code.

    1. This is final round
    2. The Participants are expected to exhibit their skills in programming.
    3. Time Limit:60 Minutes
